Thai Soup Recipe

Description

This scrumptious Thai Soup Recipe makes one serving.

Ingredients

  • Red curry paste
  • Fresh grated ginger
  • Chicken broth
  • Fish sauce
  • Brown sugar
  • Coconut milk
  • Lime juice
  • Mushrooms
  • Cilantro
  • Salt
  • Lemongrass

Directions

  1. Cook ginder, curry paste and lemongrass in oil.
  2. Stir in broth, brown sugar and fish sauce and let it simmer.
  3. Add coconut milk and mushrooms. Cook and stir until mushrooms are soft.
  4. Add shrimp and cook until no longer translucent.
  5. Stir in lime juice, add salt to taste and garnish.
