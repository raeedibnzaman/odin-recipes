Lemonade Recipe

Description

This refreshing recipe makes one serving of fresh lemonade.

Ingredients

  • Whole lemon
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Carbonated Water

Directions

  1. Squeeze lemon into a glass
  2. Add 2 tbsp of sugar
  3. Fill glass up to 2/3rd with water
  4. Stir and dissolve sugar
  5. Sprinkle a pinch of salt
  6. Pour carbonated water
  7. Garnish with a lemon slice and enjoy!
